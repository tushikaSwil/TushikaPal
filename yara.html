Yara

The YARA Voice Chat Room application is developed to ensure that everyone within all communities can interact with each other without age or gender barriers. This app provides users with other fun features also, which can be availed by purchasing artificial coins.

Overview

Engaging in activities with friends is one of the most enjoyable ways to spend free time. Yara app offers a unique way to connect with friends through chatting and gaming. With Game as destination users can build a gaming community of high-quality players with whom they can play & chat same time.

Project Goal

  • Provide best voice chat & gaming experience to users.
  • Keep your voice chat experience encrypted with options of creating private rooms and blocking unwanted entrants.
  • Let users build their own community of followers.

Problem statement

  • When we talk about games, we need to manage multiple IDs with the winning/losing amount for multiple players in real-time. As of earlier, this was a great challenge.
  • Earlier there was no theme-changing feature for users also, users were facing challenge in setting frames on their profiles which does not seem user-friendly.
  • Public and Private room creation was a challenge in app which could be a threat to privacy of users and cause inconvenience.

Solutions

  • We managed to create multiple sockets to handle real-time data.
  • We added a feature of room lock to avoid user inconvenience or privacy breach by creating a room password option that can be shared by room creator to users of their choice. Also, users can make a sub admin or co-host to control chat room activities in absence of real admin.
  • Theme changing option added in two categories like free and paid. Here paid versions require the user to have virtual coins in their user account.

Style Guide

  • I used Purple and pink gradient. This combination is visually appealing and can create an inviting and friendly atmosphere. They are often associated with creativity, warmth, and a sense of comfort.
  • This color combination can be gender-neutral and appeal to a wide range of users, which is important for inclusivity and broad user adoption.
  • For typography, I used a classical serif font.

Project Overview

This project focuses on the development of a mobile application tailored for hookah enthusiasts, aiming to revolutionize their purchasing experience. Our objective was to create an intuitive platform offering a diverse catalog of hookah products and accessories while fostering a vibrant community.

Project Goal

  • Our first goal is to prioritize the creation of an intuitive and engaging interface that simplifies the browsing, selection, and purchasing process for hookah enthusiasts.
  • Secondly, Foster a sense of community among users by incorporating social features such as reviews, ratings, and a forum for discussions around hookah experiences, tips, and trends.

Problem statement

  • Existing avenues for purchasing hookah-related products lack cohesion, making it challenging for enthusiasts to explore diverse offerings conveniently.
  • The absence of a dedicated platform catering to hookah aficionados results in a lack of communal engagement and knowledge sharing.

Style Guide

  • I used Poppins font for this application. Poppins is a highly legible and versatile sans-serif font. Its balanced letterforms and consistent design across different weights ensure clear readability, especially on digital screens.
  • I used black and white theme for this applocation. Because this theme exudes a timeless elegance that aligns well with the luxurious and sophisticated image often associated with hookah culture. This minimalist color scheme can evoke a sense of class and refinement, enhancing the perceived value of the products showcased within the app.
